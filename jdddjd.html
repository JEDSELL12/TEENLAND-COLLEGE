CLASSES of food

food is divided into several categories

  • carbohdrate
  • Carbohydrates are organic compounds made up of carbon, hydrogen, and oxygen
  • fat and oil
  • Fats are a concentrated source of energy, help in the absorption of fat-soluble vitamins (A, D, E, and K), and are important for cell structure and function.
  • vitamin
  • Vitamins are organic compounds that are necessary for various metabolic processes, including immune support, energy production, and maintaining skin, bone, and eye health.
