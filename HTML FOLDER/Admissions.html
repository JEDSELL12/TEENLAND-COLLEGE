Admissions at Teenland College

Thank you for your interest in Teenland College! We are excited to welcome new students who are ready to embark on their academic journey. Below, you’ll find all the information you need to apply for our programs.

How to Apply

The admissions process at Teenland College is simple and straightforward. Follow the steps below to complete your application:

  • Step 1: Choose the program you want to apply for (JSS1-3 or SSS1-3).
  • Step 2: Complete the online application form with all required documents, including your academic transcripts, resume, and personal statement.
  • Step 3: Submit your application and await a confirmation email from our admissions team.
  • Step 4: If your application is successful, you’ll receive an invitation for an interview or additional steps based on your program.
  • Step 5: Once accepted, complete the registration process and join Teenland College!

Admission Requirements

To be eligible for admission, applicants must meet the following criteria:

  • Completed Common Entrance
  • Applicants must bring proof of previous class
