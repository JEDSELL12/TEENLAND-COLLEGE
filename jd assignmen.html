Junior Secondary School Subjects and Their Topic 1

Mathematics

Topic 1: Number Systems and Operations

Introduction to integers, rational numbers, and operations (addition, subtraction, multiplication, division) with these numbers.

English Language

Topic 1: Parts of Speech

Introduction to the different parts of speech such as nouns, verbs, adjectives, adverbs, pronouns, prepositions, conjunctions, and interjections.

Science

Topic 1: Introduction to Science

Overview of the branches of science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology), scientific methods, and basic lab safety rules.

Social Studies

Topic 1: The Concept of Society

Understanding society, its elements, and the relationship between individuals and society.

Geography

Topic 1: Introduction to Geography

Definition of geography, its importance, and the study of the earth's features, climate, and human-environment interaction.

History

Topic 1: Introduction to History

The study of history, its significance, and the different methods of studying past events, including sources of historical information.

Civic Education

Topic 1: Introduction to Civic Education

The role of citizens in society, importance of good governance, civic rights, and responsibilities.

French

Topic 1: Basic French Vocabulary and Greetings

Introduction to basic French words, phrases, and greetings (e.g., Bonjour, Merci, etc.).

Physical Education

Topic 1: Introduction to Physical Fitness

Basic concepts of physical fitness, types of exercises, and the importance of physical activity for health.

Agricultural Science

Topic 1: Introduction to Agriculture

Basic concepts in agriculture, types of farming, and the importance of agriculture to society.

Business Studies

Topic 1: Introduction to Business

Basic concepts in business, types of business organizations, and the importance of entrepreneurship.

Computer Studies

Topic 1: Introduction to Computers

Basic computer components, types of computers, and how to operate a computer.

Home Economics

Topic 1: Introduction to Home Economics

Basic home management skills, food and nutrition, and importance of a balanced diet.

Basic Technology

Topic 1: Introduction to Technical Drawing

Basic lines, shapes, and symbols used in technical drawing, as well as the tools and equipment needed for drawing.
